Overview of the Party Package

The Foundation Party package introduces the PARTICIPATION, PARTY, PARTY_RELATIONSHIP, BASE_ROLE, and BASE_ACTOR classes. These classes are used to represent the participation of entities in clinical activities and the roles assumed during such participations. For instance, a person (Entity) may participate (PARTICIPATION) in a clinical activity (Statement Act Topic) as a patient (ROLE)
