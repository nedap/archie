Overview of the Adverse Reaction Package and Related Inheritance Hierarchy (EXPERIMENTAL).

Package for classes that pertain to AdverseReaction (green in diagram).

An adverse reaction is an untoward finding that results from exposure to a substance and is typically a manifestation of an underlying adverse sensitivity to a substance.
