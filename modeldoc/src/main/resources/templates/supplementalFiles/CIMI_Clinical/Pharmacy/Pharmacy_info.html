CIMI_Clinical.Pharmacy

Overview of the Pharmacy Package

The Pharmacy package contains classes related to the administration and dispense of medications.

Pharmacy classes can be used to represent Medication Administration, Medication Statement, Medication Request, and Medication Dispense. They are derived from archetypes on the following clinical statements, topics, and contexts:

  • Medication Administration: ClinicalStatement<MedicationAct,(Non)Performance>
  • Medication Dispense: ClinicalStatement<MedicationAct,MedicationDispensePerformance>
  • Medication Request: ClinicalStatement<MedicationAct,MedicationOrder>
  • Medication Statement: ClinicalStatement<MedicationAct,(Non)Performance>
